  • Currently, I am a mental health social worker in Chongqing, China, for a community-based rehabilitation programme of people with mental illness.
  • I obtained a Master's degree in Social Work from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in Wuhan, China, in 2022 and a Bachelor's degree in Social Work from Fujian Medical University in Fuzhou, China, in 2020.
  • Over the past 6 years, I have honed my social work skills by participating in a wide range of projects, especially in the clinical setting. And I have developed my research skills by working as a research assistant in a project focused on the caregivers of people with Alzheimer’s disease and other diseases.
  • My main interests focus on the field of caregiver research, analysis, and mental health. Currently, I am focusing on major surveys regarding the attitude and behaviour of people with mental illness and the needs of family caregivers, their skills, and their experiential knowledge about the illness. A future project also aims to identify the patterns of stigma, burden and family functioning regarding patients' age and disease, as well as indications of the underlying causes.
  • Now, I would like to challenge myself by applying for the PhD program.
